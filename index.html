About TED

About TED

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading.

Started as a conference in California 33 years ago, TED has grown to support those world-changing ideas with many initiatives. TED conferences connect the brilliant minds of our world, giving them the chance to share their ideas within 18 minutes or less. The TEDx initiative grants licenses to people around the world to organize these events on a smaller scale in their communities with TED Talks and live speakers.



TEDx

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxLPU where x=independently organized TED event At our TEDx Event, TED Talk videos and live speakers will combine to bring together innovations within a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.

Theme | Ideate. Inculcate. Promulgate.

Theme | Ideate. Inculcate. Promulgate.

From Madagascar to Mumbai, New York to New Delhi, we have ideas that permeate an endless sea of people, owing to a diverse range of mediums. The web, being the most lucrative. And with the pace of the age as it is, ideas have successfully managed to break boundaries and seep in through human consciousness across the globe.

The travelling time of ideas has reduced keeping in line with the rapid globalisation, leading to an unimaginable rate of growth, but at the same time been the cause behind large-scale ignorance too, ironically.

Ideas should hold gravitas, some substance in order to naturally pave their way through and through, thus convincing an individual to truly, intrinsically believe in the truth, and thus, imbibe it naturally. Since it isn't just vital to spread an idea as much as it is to ensure that it disseminates, to facilitate the second stage of the process of change.

The TED foundation through it's conferences has always worked to create an open space, where a person from any part of the world can gather insight into the global affairs through ideas that are spread by trailblazers and pioneers from different backgrounds.

And in keeping that torch burning, to inspire and create the seeds of change through groundbreaking ideas that have managed to successfully change the order of things in the world, we bring to you TEDxLPU 2017.
